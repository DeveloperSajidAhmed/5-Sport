Golves

Cricket gloves are essential protective equipment worn by batsmen in cricket to protect their hands from the impact of the hard cricket ball. They are also designed to provide a good grip on the bat and improve comfort and control while batting.

Helmet

Cricket helmets are crucial pieces of protective equipment worn by batsmen to shield their head from the impact of a fast-moving cricket ball.

Bat

A cricket bat is a specialized piece of equipment used by batters in the sport of cricket. It is a flat-fronted willow wood blade attached to a cane handle. The laws of cricket dictate that the blade must be made from willow wood and cannot be wider than 4.25 inches (10.8 cm). The entire bat, including the handle, cannot be more than 38 inches (96.5 cm) long.
